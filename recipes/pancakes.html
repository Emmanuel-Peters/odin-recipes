Pancakes

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Maple Syrup (Prefer from cape breton)
  • Baking Powder
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Natural Greek Yogurt

Steps

  1. Combine all wet ingredients except MS
  2. Combine dry ingredients
  3. once both are mixxed, combine everything into bowl
  4. Frying pan med heat with butter
  5. add 125mL mix or whatever suits
  6. Check bottom, if done flip
  7. Serve with yogurt, and MS
