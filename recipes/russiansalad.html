Russian Salad

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Tuna
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes
  2. Chop potatoes
  3. Boil potatoes
  4. Add eggs to same pot as potatoes, and boil for 10ish mins
  5. When potatoes are soft enough, drain and mash with butter and tuna
  6. De shell eggs, serve quickly. Can also add hot sauce
