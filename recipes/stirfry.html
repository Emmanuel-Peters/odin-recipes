Stirfry

Ingredients

  • Egg noodles
  • veggie mix
  • Sesame oil
  • Tofu
  • Some kind of sauce

Steps

  1. put oil in pan and heat to high
  2. whilst waiting, cut and drain tofu
  3. fry the tofu hard until cripsy
  4. Add veggies, continue stirring, turn heat down
  5. Add noodles and sauce, stir
